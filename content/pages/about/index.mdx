Trophy is a web3 reputation protocol for anyone to manage their Soulbound tokens (SBTs).

Here are 3 good reasons to own Soulbound Tokens (SBTs).

1. Build Reputation: Soulbound Tokens (SBTs) are socially verifiable proofs of credentials, commitments and achievements that establish provenance and reputation.

2. Unlock Opportunities: Soulbound Tokens (SBTs) can help individuals and organizations get new businesses, jobs, join communities, rent apartments, gain voting power and unlock many more opportunities.

3. Own Your Greatness: Own Your Greatness with Soulbound Tokens(SBTs) that shows your identity, achievements and reputation virtually and in real life.
