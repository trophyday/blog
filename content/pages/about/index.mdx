Our mission is to help people and businesses own their greatness to unlock opportunities.

We are all about Soulbound tokens (SBTs). 

Trophy will be the web3 protocol for anyone to create, issue and manage SBTs.

Soulbound Tokens, or SBTs, are lifetime digital assets that are provable commitments, credentials, and affiliations. They can be used to represent both identity and reputation of their owners.

Here are three good reasons to own SBTs.

1. **Prove Accomplishments:** You receive Soulbound Tokens(SBTs) for completing a degree, a earning professional certification, working for a company, repaying a loan, winning an award, and so on. Soulbound Tokens (SBTs) are provable and socially verifiable lifetime accomplishments.

2. **Build Reputation:** A protofolio of Soulbound Tokens(SBTs) shows your identity, achievements and reputation both online and in real life.

3. **Unlock Opportunities:** Soulbound Tokens (SBTs) can help individuals and organizations get new businesses, new jobs, join communities, get credits, rent apartments, gain voting power and unlock many more opportunities.
